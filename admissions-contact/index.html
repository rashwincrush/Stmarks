Admissions & Contact

At St. Mark's, we welcome families who share our commitment to holistic education. Our admissions process is designed to ensure the best fit for both the child and our community. We look forward to partnering with you in your child's educational journey.

What to Expect

Our admissions process focuses on understanding your child's needs and ensuring they thrive in our environment. We encourage parents to visit the school, meet with our faculty, and experience our community firsthand. For specific details on requirements and procedures, please contact us directly.
