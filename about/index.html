Quality Assurance

Our Teacher Accountability System

Five checkpoints that keep every lesson, note, and assessment consistent, accurate, and student-ready.

5 checkpoint loop
3 sign-offs per paper
100% checked notes
Step 1

Checking of Teacher's Notes

All notes are submitted for approval to ensure factual and grammatical accuracy before they reach students.

Step 2

Inspection of Classwork

Notebooks are corrected by teachers and cross-inspected by academic heads for perfection.

Step 3

Fixation of Academic Target

Term-wise targets motivate, train, and support teachers to help every child succeed.

Step 4

Inspection of Question Papers

Each paper is checked for content accuracy and signed off by teacher, vice principal, and typist.

Step 5

Answer Paper Evaluation

Random checks after every exam assess performance and ensure correction quality.
