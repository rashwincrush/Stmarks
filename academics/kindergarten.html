Welcome to a World of Wonder

Happy, enthusiastic teachers welcome children into vibrant, colourful classes. Through a play-way, immersive method we introduce English and Tamil basics, numeracy, general awareness (science topics), and phonetics—alongside daily physical activities and art & craft to build fine and gross motor skills.

What children learn here

Play-way, immersive learning that builds early skills and confidence.

  • Basics of English & Tamil
  • Numeracy (number concepts)
  • General awareness (science topics)
  • Phonetics
  • Daily physical activities + art & craft (fine & gross motor skills)

KG Activities

Each day in our Kindergarten is filled with a mix of structured learning and joyful experiences that help children grow socially, emotionally, and intellectually.

  • Morning circle time with rhymes, stories, and conversations to build confidence and communication.
  • Theme-based activities and projects that introduce colours, shapes, seasons, festivals, and community helpers.
  • Hands-on art and craft sessions to develop creativity and fine motor skills.
  • Indoor and outdoor play that encourages sharing, teamwork, and physical development.
  • Regular celebrations and special days that make school a happy and memorable place for young children.
