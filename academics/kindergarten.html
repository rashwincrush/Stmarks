Kindergarten

Classes: Pre-KG, Junior KG, Senior KG

About Kindergarten

The first step of education of a child begins with their kindergarten classes and the enormity of its significance is well understood by our KG department. Happy enthusiastic teachers welcome children into vibrant, colorful classes. The play-way, immersive method is adopted to introduce our children to the basics of languages (English and Tamil), Numeracy (numbers and number concepts), general awareness (science topics) and phonetics. The children also participate in daily physical activities, art and craft sessions to improve their fine and gross motor skills. A department of celebration, the children, the teachers and the parents get together to celebrate a myriad of days including "Color Day", "Grandparents Day", "Children's Day" to name a few.

Subjects & Focus

  • Languages: English and Tamil basics
  • Numeracy: Numbers and number concepts
  • General Awareness: Science topics
  • Phonetics: Sound recognition and formation
  • Physical Activities: Daily exercises to develop motor skills
  • Art & Craft: Creative sessions for fine motor skill development

Highlights & Activities

Our Kindergarten program emphasizes play-based learning and celebration of various occasions to make learning fun and memorable.

  • Play-way and immersive learning methods
  • Vibrant and colorful classroom environment
  • Daily physical activities and art sessions
  • Celebration of special days like Color Day, Grandparents Day, Children's Day
  • Focus on developing fine and gross motor skills
  • Introduction to basic concepts through fun activities

Important Notes

  • The Pre-KG children have half-day classes only
  • Saturdays are holidays by default for the kindergarten department
