Middle School

Classes: VI, VII and VIII

About Middle School

The middle school is the most transformative phase of a child's school life. The children are introduced to physics, chemistry and biology for the first time. Social sciences are also studied in depth as individual subjects of History and Geography. The teachers and the Vice principal of the department meet the children when they are in the last term of class V to allay any fears they might have about the transition to middle school. Class six does not start with lessons immediately, the teachers cover the basic concepts of the new subjects and familiarise the children with them, before the syllabus is tackled. The children are gradually streamlined into spending extensive time with their books actively.

Subjects & Focus

  • English: Advanced language and literature
  • Tamil: Regional language proficiency
  • Mathematics: Advanced numerical concepts
  • Physics: Introduction to physical sciences
  • Chemistry: Basic chemical principles
  • Botany: Plant biology
  • History: Historical studies
  • Geography: Geographical knowledge
  • Science: Integrated science concepts
  • Computer Science: Computing skills
  • Hindi: Additional language
  • Communicative English: Communication skills

Highlights & Activities

Our Middle School program focuses on smooth transition, academic rigor, and balanced development.

  • Supportive transition from primary to middle school
  • Introduction to advanced science subjects
  • In-depth study of History and Geography
  • Gradual adaptation to increased academic demands
  • Curricular events: learning-based activities, intramural games, fine art competitions
  • General counselling sessions for adolescence
  • Balance between academic learning, cultural enrichment, and physical fitness
