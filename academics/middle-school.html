Navigating the Transition

Middle school is where students go deeper. Sciences are introduced as Physics, Chemistry, and Biology; Social Science expands into History and Geography. The structure, resources, and guidance reduce transition anxiety and build confidence.

What changes in Middle School

  • Sciences become Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Social Science expands into History and Geography
