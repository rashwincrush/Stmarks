Primary

Classes: III, IV and V

About Primary

According to the NEP 2020, the classes III, IV and V, act as the preparatory phase for the higher classes. The experiential learning and the child centric classes ensure that this objective is met by our children. Children are encouraged to ask questions, explore their interests and implement their learnings in a practical manner.

Subjects & Focus

  • English: Language and communication skills
  • Tamil: Regional language proficiency
  • Mathematics: Numerical concepts and problem-solving
  • Science: Basic scientific principles
  • Social Science: Understanding society and history
  • Computer Science: Introduction to computing
  • Hindi: Additional language learning
  • Communicative English: Enhanced communication

Highlights & Activities

Our Primary program emphasizes experiential learning, curiosity, and practical implementation of knowledge.

  • Experiential and child-centric learning
  • Encouragement to ask questions and explore interests
  • Annual Talent Week
  • Science Project Day
  • Individual and group projects for teamwork and presentation skills
  • Beyond Academic Initiative: handwriting, aptitude, art & craft, value education
  • Daily class assemblies
