High School

Classes: IX and X

About High School

Our children's sojourn in the high school department culminates with their first ever public board exams. This daunting proposition is made simple for our high schoolers by our efficient high school teachers. This experienced team ensures that the children are given feasible daily, weekly, monthly targets and are guided individually, to achieve these targets effectively.

Subjects & Focus

High school curriculum builds on previous learning with emphasis on board exam preparation.

  • English: Literature and language proficiency
  • Tamil: Regional language
  • Mathematics: Advanced mathematical concepts
  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Social Science: History, Geography, Civics
  • Computer Science: Advanced computing
  • Hindi: Additional language
  • Communicative English: Communication skills

Highlights & Activities

Our High School program prepares students for board exams while fostering talents and career awareness.

  • Individual guidance for board exam preparation
  • Feasible daily, weekly, monthly targets
  • Theme-based activities and intra-department competitions
  • Multiple revision and model exams for Class X
  • Career guidance program starting from Class IX
  • Guidance on various careers and career identification
