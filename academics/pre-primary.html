Pre-Primary

Classes: I and II

About Pre-Primary

The pre-primary department comprising of classes I and II start the formal part of your child's foundational phase of education. The fresh kindergarten graduates follow a structured, comprehensive syllabus along with many co-curricular and extracurricular events for two years which make them ready for their future academic exploits. The meticulously selected books, the well informed, involved teachers, and the innovative learning methodologies ensure that each day in the department is a day of active learning and development.

Subjects & Focus

  • English: Language development and communication
  • Tamil: Regional language skills
  • Mathematics: Basic numerical concepts
  • Science: Introduction to the natural world
  • Hindi: Additional language learning
  • Communicative English: Enhanced communication skills
  • Computer Science: Basic computer literacy
  • Physical Education: Weekly classes for physical activity

Highlights & Activities

Our Pre-Primary program focuses on active learning, development, and preparing children for future academic success.

  • Structured and comprehensive syllabus
  • Co-curricular and extracurricular events
  • Meticulously selected educational materials
  • Innovative learning methodologies
  • "No-bag-day Saturdays" with reading, art, craft, and aptitude exercises
  • Weekly Physical Education classes
