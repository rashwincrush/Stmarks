Building Strong Foundations

The pre-primary stage is the first step into formal education. Students are taught by trained, committed teachers with a structured syllabus and ample co-curricular exposure. Weekly physical education keeps children active and sharp, and a student-friendly environment makes transition smooth.

Academic focus

Students study English, Tamil, Mathematics, Science and also have Hindi, communicative English, and computer science as part of the timetable.

  • Core subjects (English, Tamil, Mathematics, Science)
  • Additional Hindi, Communicative English, Computer Science
