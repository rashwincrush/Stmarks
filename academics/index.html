Our Academic Programs

At St. Mark's, we offer a comprehensive educational journey from Kindergarten to Higher Secondary, ensuring holistic development and academic excellence.

School Structure

St. Mark's Matriculation Higher Secondary school is run by the St. Mark's educational trust and is duly recognised by the government of Tamil Nadu. The school follows the Samacheer board and also adopts the best practices from the various educational boards in India. The school is split into the following six departments, each headed by its own vice principal:
