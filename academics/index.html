Six Departments, One Journey

St. Mark's is organised into six specialised departments, each led by a vice principal. From Kindergarten to Higher Secondary, students experience clear transitions, age-appropriate teaching, and department-wise care at every stage.

  • Commerce
  • Accountancy
  • Economics
  • Computer Applications

Group 4

Commerce + Business Math

  • Commerce
  • Accountancy
  • Economics
  • Business Mathematics

Note: English is compulsory as the first language; second language choice is Tamil or French for all groups.
