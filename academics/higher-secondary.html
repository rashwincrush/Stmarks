Charting the Path Forward

Group selection counselling post-Class X, strong board prep, leadership opportunities, and guidance for college and careers.

Group selection counselling (post Class X)

After Class X results, the school conducts one-on-one counselling with students and parents to choose the best-fit group.

Academic support

  • Optional daily test schedule to improve scores across subjects
  • Revision + mock exams to face XI/XII board exams with confidence

Leadership & recognition

Student leaders are identified and given roles (pupil leader, sports captain, cultural secretary, etc.).

Groups offered (XI & XII)

Note: English is compulsory as the first language; second language choice is Tamil or French.

  • Group 1: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science
  • Group 2: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology
  • Group 3: Commerce, Accountancy, Economics, Computer Applications
  • Group 4: Commerce, Accountancy, Economics, Business Mathematics
