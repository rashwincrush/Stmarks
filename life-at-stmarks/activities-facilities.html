Infrastructure

Facilities that support daily learning

Practical, well-kept spaces in Chromepet: clear labs for hands-on practice, a quiet library routine, steady device access, and AV lessons that make concepts easier to grasp.

🧪 Lab safety first: supervised practicals

Science Labs

Separate labs for physics, chemistry, and biology. Supervised practicals for Classes X–XII; Class VIII–IX observe demos to build safe, hands-on confidence.

Library & Reading

Weekly, supervised reading hours. Updated shelves for fiction and non-fiction; short book reflections to keep students accountable.

Computer Access

Device time from Class III upward. Typing and productivity basics first; starter coding (HTML, C, Python) introduced in middle school.

Audio-Visual Room

Projector-led sessions to simplify tougher topics. Used across subjects for demos, documentaries, and occasional guest lessons.
